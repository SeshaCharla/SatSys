\subsection{Variance in $\eta_{sat}$ prediction}
The variance in the $\eta_{sat}$ prediction for the given time-step is predicted using the distribution of the paremter estimates. Form (\ref{eqn::regression}), we have,
\begin{align}
        \Var\lr{\eta_{sat}(k)} &= \phi_{sat}(k)^T \Var\lr{\theta_{sat}} \phi_{sat}(k) \notag \\
        \implies \sigma_{\eta_{sat}}(k) &= \phi_{sat}(k)^T I^{-1}(\theta_{sat}) \phi_{sat}(k)
\end{align}
