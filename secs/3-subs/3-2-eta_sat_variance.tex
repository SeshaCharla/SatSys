% \subsection{Variance in $\eta_{sat}$ prediction}
The variance in the $\eta_F^{sat}$ prediction for the given time-step is obtained by propagating the uncertainity of
paramter estimates (\ref{eqn::theta_dist}). Using, (\ref{eqn::regression}), we have,
\begin{align}
        \Var\lrb{\eta_F^{sat}(k)} &= \phi_{sat}(k)^T I^{-1}(\theta_{sat}) \phi_{sat}(k)
\end{align}
